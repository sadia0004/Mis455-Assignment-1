Digital Art : Digital art is created using digital tools like drawing tablets, software(e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate), or graphic design applications. It encompasses a wide variety of styles and forms, from realistic paintings to abstract designs and even 3D modeling. Digital art allows for extensive creative control and flexibility. Artists can experiment with textures, colors, and styles more easily than with traditional mediums, and it can be used for anything from digital painting to animation or game design.

Mandala : A mandala is a geometric design or pattern, typically circular, representing the universe or wholeness. Mandalas are often intricate and symmetrical, with complex, repeating patterns radiating from a central point Normally drawn with pens, markers, or digital tools; sometimes filled with vibrant colors.

Face Sketch: A face sketch is a simplified, often black-and-white, representation of a human face, capturing key features such as the eyes, nose, lips, and overall structure with minimal details. It is a popular form of portrait drawing, focusing on the proportions and features that make the subject recognizable. Typically done with pencils (graphite or charcoal), pens, or sometimes digitally using a stylus on a drawing tablet.

Colorful and bright drawings: Colorful and bright drawings are vibrant artworks that use a wide range of vivid, eye-catching colors. These drawings can be realistic or abstract and are often designed to invoke joy, energy, or playfulness. This type of art is often used to create an uplifting mood, convey positivity, or explore the beauty of color. It can be found in children's art, comic illustrations, or modern abstract works. Bold use of colors, strong contrasts, cheerful and engaging compositions.

Doodle: Doodling is the act of drawing simple, often abstract, or whimsical shapes and designs, usually without a specific plan. Doodles are typically spontaneous and done casually, often while the artist's mind is focused elsewhere. A form of self-expression, doodles often happen during moments of boredom or distraction, but they can also be intricate and creative pieces of art in their own right. Abstract shapes, repetitive patterns, simple figures, and random designs.
